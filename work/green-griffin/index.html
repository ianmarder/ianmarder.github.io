The Green Griffin Tavern

Building the foundational visual language for a tabletop gaming brand designed to host immersive, community-driven adventures.

The Strategy

Green Griffin Tavern is an up-and-coming brand in the TTRPG space specializing in professional game-running. The project began with a blank slate other than the name, requiring a deep dive into aesthetics, brand signatures, and voice. We moved through several iterations to find a name that felt both legendary and welcoming.

Role

Lead Creative / Brand Strategist

Industry

TTRPG / Tabletop Gaming

Services

The Process: From Idea to Griffin

The journey to the 'Green Griffin' involved exploring various brand signatures and lore-friendly vibes. The goal was to create a mark that could be tangible—something to be stamped on a character sheet, animated on a website, or burnt into a wooden stein.

Hot, cold, and fresh

To evoke the 'warm tavern vibe,' we leaned into a palette of deep hearth-side oranges, charcoal greys, and ember glows. The color system is designed to give players a feeling of stepping out of the cold and into a safe, warm space for storytelling. All while the dark, cold blues lurk outside ready to ensare our players.
And... well, y'know. A green. For the griffin.
