The Playbook

For the 2026 Revenue Kickoff, we transformed a standard corporate meeting into a live "SportsCenter" broadcast. I architected the entire visual system—from the digital "desk" segments to physical assets—serving as the lead designer, showrunner, and even the "announcer" to keep the energy high.

The Vibe

Key Deliverables

Broadcast Package: Intro & Pause Screens

The event relied on "the flip"—switching seamlessly between the news desk and remote reporters. I designed motion graphics loops and high-energy pause screens to maintain the immersion during transitions.

The ID System: Abstract Brand Shapes

Leveraging core brand geometry into dynamic sports-style graphics.

The Content Engine: Marketing, Sales, & Product

I built and art-directed the entire slide ecosystem for every department. This involved custom template architecture that allowed speakers to deliver complex data while staying inside the "AchieversCenter" broadcast aesthetic.

Voiceover & Comedic Styling

To lean into the parody, I provided the announcer voiceover and comedic direction for the segments, bridging the gap between high-level corporate messaging and engaging entertainment.
